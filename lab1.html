Session 1: 3D Printing a Keychain

Follow the steps below to design and fabricate your 3D printed keychain

Part 1: Introduction & Safety

3D printing is an additive manufacturing process. You will learn advanced modeling, assembly, and fabrication skills. Follow all safety instructions while using machines and tools.

  1. Learn advanced 3D modeling Techniques (Revolve, Sweep, Warp).
  2. Design for Multimaterial 3D Printing.
  3. CAD a simple rotating element around a shaft.
  4. CAD a threaded object.
  5. Make a Mechanical Assembly.
  6. Render a 3D Model in Fusion360.
  7. Export files and prepare them for 3D printing.

Fabrication Quest of the Day:

  1. Assemble your Rotating Keychain.
  2. Post-process your 3D print.

Software & Hardware

  1. Software: Fusion360
  2. Machines: 3D Printer

Part 2: Sketch and Dimension Parametrically

(Sketching Keychain Shaft, 5 minutes)

  1. Draw a circle at the center (0,0).
  2. Dimension the first circle with “shaft_outer_d”.
  3. Exit Sketch, and rename the Sketch to "shaft-circle".

Part 3: Extruding the Sketch

(Give height to the shaft, 5 minutes)

  1. Click on Create.
  2. Select the Extrude Tool.
  3. Under Profile, select the area formed by the circle from your "shaft-circle" sketch.
  4. Set Direction to One Side.
  5. Type Distance as “shaft_length_3b” or "shaft_length_4b".
  6. Choose operation as New Component.
  7. Right click component and rename it "Shaft".

Part 4: Making a Threaded Hole

(7 minutes)

  1. Toggle "Shaft-circle" sketch back on.
  2. Go to Create > Hole Tool.
  3. Under Face, select top face of the cylinder.
  4. Set Hole Type to “Simple”, Tap Type “Tapped”, Thread Offset “Full”, Drill Point “Angle”.
  5. Depth: "thread_depth", Size: 7mm, Designation: M7mmx1mm.
  6. Have Modeled checked ON, click OK.

Checkoff 2: Show your shaft with threaded hole to your instructor.

Part 5: Creating Profile of Keychain Holder Head (Remix Optional)

(20 minutes)

  1. Create Axis Through Cylinder (Construct → Axis Through Cylinder).
  2. Create Plane at Angle (Construct → Plane at Angle → 0°).
  3. Create new sketch on plane.
  4. Project top circle perimeter and sketch top holder profile.
  5. Finish sketch.

Checkoff 3: Show & explain your sketched profile to your instructor.

Part 6: Revolving Radially Symmetric Geometry

(5 minutes)

  1. Create > Revolve.
  2. Select profile and axis of symmetry.
  3. Set angle 360°.
  4. Click OK.

Part 7: Making Hole for Keychain

(5 minutes)

  1. Create tangent plane.
  2. Sketch circle where hole should be.
  3. Dimension circle (4mm).
  4. Extrude cut circle, symmetric direction.

Checkoff 4: Show your shaft's keychain hole to your instructor.
