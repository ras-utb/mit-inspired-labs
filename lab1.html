Part (2): Sketch and Dimension Parametrically (Sketching Keychain Shaft) (5 minutes)

  • Draw a circle at the center (0,0).

Part (3): Extruding the Sketch (5 minutes)

  • Click on Create.
  • Select the Extrude Tool.
  • Under Profile, select the area formed by the circle from your "shaft-circle" sketch.
  • Set the Direction to One Side.
  • Type the Distance as ‚Äúshaft_length_3b‚Äù or "shaft_length_4b".
  • Choose the operation as New Component.
  • Click Ok to create the solid cylinder.
  • Rename the component to "Shaft".

Part (4): Making a Threaded Hole (7 minutes)

Video Link

  • Toggle "Shaft-circle" sketch back on.
  • Go to Create > Hole Tool.
  • Select top face of cylinder.
  • Set Reference to center of circle sketch.
  • Set Hole Type to Simple and Tap Type to Tapped.
  • Set Thread Offset to Full, Drill Point to Angle, Depth to "thread_depth", Size 7mm, Designation M7mmx1mm.
  • Have Modeled checked ON.
  • Click Ok.

Checkoff 2: Show your shaft with its threaded hole to your instructor.

Part (5): Creating Profile of Keychain Holder Head (üö®REMIX OPTIONALüö®) (20 minutes)

Video Link

  • Create Axis Through Cylinder: Construct > Axis Through Cylinder.
  • Create Plane at Angle: Construct > Plane at Angle > angle 0.
  • Sketch the profile on the new plane.
  • Project geometry from cylinder top circle.
  • Ensure sketch is symmetric and closed.
  • Finish Sketch > Revolve to create top holder.

Checkoff 3: Show & explain your sketched profile to your instructor.

Part (6): Revolving Radially Symmetric Geometry (5 minutes)

Video Link

  • Revolve Command: Create > Revolve.
  • Select Profile to revolve.
  • Select axis of symmetry (cylinder axis).
  • Set Angle to 360¬∞ (or desired angle for partial revolution).
  • Click OK to complete operation.

Part (7): Making Hole for Keychain (5 minutes)

Video Link

  • Create a Tangent Plane: Construct > Tangent Plane.
  • Sketch a circle for the hole.
  • Dimension circle to 4mm.
  • Extrude Cut the Hole: Create > Extrude > Cut Symmetric.

Checkoff 4: Show your shaft's keychain hole to your instructor.
