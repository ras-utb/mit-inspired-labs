Course Overview

This student-led lab series at UTB is inspired by the AUBH MIT MISTI program. Students explore hands-on STEM projects including 3D modeling, rapid prototyping, electronics, and interactive GUIs through mini-projects and instructor check-ins.

About

This series is organized by students who previously participated in the AUBH MIT-inspired labs. Our mission is to provide UTB students with experiential STEM learning opportunities, fostering creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills through hands-on projects.
