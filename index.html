UTB Student-Led Labs: Course Overview

This student-led lab series at UTB is inspired by the AUBH MIT MISTI program. We aim to provide hands-on STEM learning experiences, transforming traditional concepts into interactive, real-world projects. Each lab engages participants in creative design and fabrication techniques using mechanical, electrical, or physical/chemical methods.

Students explore foundational technologies and fabrication methods including 3D modeling, rapid prototyping (3D printing and laser cutting), sensing and electronics, and graphical user interface development. Labs include individual mini-projects, instructor check-ins, and in-class reviews to guide progress.
